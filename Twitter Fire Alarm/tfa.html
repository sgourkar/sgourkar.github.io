Twitter Fire Alarm

Living in Southern California comes with a lot of perks as well as challenges. Alongside the sunny weather and beaches comes also the seasonal winds, dry weather, and unfortunately also fires. This past fall we've had quite a few fires in this area, and so the goal of project is to create a home alarm for whenever there are any mentions for fires on twitter from various fire departments. I'm just getting started on this, and I'll be posting updates as they happen.
