About Me

I am a Biomedical Engineer with a passion for user-centered design thinking. As a working professional in the field of clinical research, I learned to have an appreciation for data analytics through working on clinical data for various medical journal publications. Through this interest and my exposure to a variety of hospital and outpatient environments, I decided to go back to school in order to earn my Master of Engineering degree from Boston University. Through the Late Entry Accelerated Program (LEAP) I was exposed to a variety of awesome ideas and tools like signal processing, machine learning, and rapid prototyping that have together fostered in me an interest to make things. This is my website where I will share progress on some projects/interests of mine. Thanks for visiting!
