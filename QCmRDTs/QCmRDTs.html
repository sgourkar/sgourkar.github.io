Imaging mRDTs

Here you can see a Raspbery Pi 3 device encased in a Pimoroni case. This was used to image a number of malaria rapid diagnostic tests (mRDTs). Configured with the Raspian Jesse operating system, and programmed with python, the image acquisition system was created.
